Gabriel Bell-Gam is the Founder/Director of the Bell-Gam Digital Cultural Heritage Center, specializing in the documentation and preservation of cultural heritage using advanced digital technologies like photogrammetry, 3D modeling, and VR/AR. With extensive experience in curatorial practices and international collaborations, he leads impactful projects such as the Silent Heritage Project, aligning his work with SDG 11.4 to promote sustainable cultural heritage management.
